## How Much Money Can You Really Make?

Realistically:

- Most users earn **$5 to $50 per month**
- More is possible when combining apps
- Using only one app usually leads to very low results

When I started, earnings were small. Over time, I saw that consistency matters more than chasing unrealistic app claims.

## How to Tell If an App Is Legit

Before using any app, check:

- Real user reviews
- Clear payout methods (PayPal, gift cards, bank transfer)
- Time in market and credibility

Avoid apps that promise instant large profits with no explanation.

## Tips to Maximize Your Earnings

The most effective strategy is to combine app types:

- 1 passive app (like Honeygain)
- 2 survey/task apps
- 1 optional extra app for bonus earnings

This combination creates more stable monthly results than relying on one platform.
