“Passive income” on a phone is usually **low-touch**, not **zero-effort forever**. You might need to keep an app installed, occasionally check settings, or meet payout thresholds.

## Types of passive-ish phone earners

- **Bandwidth sharing**: Runs in the background; payouts are typically slow.
- **Lock screen rewards**: Small credits over time; often discontinued or changed.
- **Data panels**: Invite-only in many cases; higher trust requirements.

## What is realistic

If an app is truly passive, earnings are usually **small**. The winning approach is to pair passive earners with active earners (surveys/tasks) so your monthly total is less volatile.

## Safety checklist

- Avoid apps that request risky permissions without a clear reason
- Prefer known brands with published policies
- Do not sideload APKs from random sites

## FAQ

### Is passive income taxable?

In the US, taxable income rules depend on your situation. Keep records and ask a tax professional if amounts become meaningful.

### Why is my passive app earning $0?

Demand varies. Some networks pay more in certain regions or seasons.
