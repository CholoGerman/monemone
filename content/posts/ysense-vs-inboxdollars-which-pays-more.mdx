**Which pays more?** It depends on your demographics, offer inventory that week, and whether you prefer surveys, games, or mixed tasks. Instead of chasing a single winner, think in terms of **where your time converts best**.

## ySense (typical strengths)

- Strong fit for users who like **surveys and microtasks**
- Works well as a **second survey app** in a stack

## InboxDollars (typical strengths)

- Broad mix: surveys, games, paid email, and offers
- Can be useful if you like **variety** more than pure surveys

## How to compare them fairly (7-day test)

- Track **time spent** and **cash out value** for the same daily window
- Hold your setup constant (same device, stable IP, honest profiling)
- Pick one primary goal: fastest cash-out vs highest total per hour

## FAQ

### Should I use both?

Often yes, but not at the same time in one sitting. Rotate sessions to reduce fatigue and screenouts.

### What is the biggest risk?

Offers with unclear terms. Read requirements before you start.
