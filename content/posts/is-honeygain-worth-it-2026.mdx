Honeygain shares a small portion of your internet bandwidth with its network and pays you when your device is online. For most people, it is **not** a meaningful primary income. It can still be useful as a **background** earner if your expectations stay low.

## Who it is for

- People who want **set-and-forget** earnings
- Users comfortable running an always-on app on Wi‑Fi
- Anyone okay with **slow** balance growth

## Who it is not for

- Anyone expecting fast cash the same week
- People on strict mobile data caps
- Users who do not want third-party traffic routed through their connection

## Realistic expectations (US)

Earnings vary heavily by network demand and how often your device is active. Think in terms of **small side income**, not rent money. Combining Honeygain with survey apps usually works better than relying on one app alone.

## Privacy and safety basics

- Read Honeygain’s terms and privacy policy.
- Prefer running on networks you control.
- Keep the app updated.

## FAQ

### Can Honeygain replace a job?

No. Treat it as optional pocket money.

### Does it drain battery?

It can if it runs aggressively in the background; many users keep it on a home Wi‑Fi device.
